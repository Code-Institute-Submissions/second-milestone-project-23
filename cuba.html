Cuba

8 days

Cuba is a country that doesn’t allow evangelism. It’s very restricted so it’s difficult to have campaigns or bible teaching conferences. What is allowed however is dancing and drama. The Bible says that we should use whatever gift we have received to serve others (1 Peter 4:10). If you love serving God through the creative gifts he’s given you, this trip is for you. We will be having 2 full days of workshop and on the third day we will go to the streets of Havana and do a performance. Last time we went we had people between the ages of 6 and 63 join our workshops and we had a blast! We also had the opportunity to speak to many people during and after the performances. We will also be given the youth service and Sunday service to preach and give testimonies.

Below is an idea of how the trip will look like, but may vary slightly depending on the creative gifts God has given each member of the team. All meals will be provided apart from the day out in Havana. If this sounds like your kind of trip, enquire below and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible.

Arrive in Havana, Cuba. A minibus will pick us up from the airport and take us to the place of residency a little outside Havana. This is the church residency, specifically made for missionaries. Late dinner/snack will be provided.

Meet with the Havana team. This day will be spent preparing for the workshops and seeing the venue where we will be rehearsing.

We start bright and early this day with breakfast, a bus will arrive and take us in to Havana where the workshops will begin at 9:30 where we start off learning an easy flashmob dance. At 10 participants will be divided into groups and be taught different dances/dramas. At 12 we break for lunch where we as a team will minister through testimonies. We start again at 13 pm switching the dance and drama groups. We finish the day at 15:30 and head back to our residence.

Same schedule as the first day workshop apart from training finishing at 14:30. The last hour will be spent doing a run-through with all the groups. Evening time we will serve in the youth service.

We head in to Havana early, do a final run-through and start our performance at 11 am. At 13 pm we finish with lunch together with everyone. Pictures and bye bye!

Sunday service where we will serve with testimonies and a preaching as well as one performance.

Free day out in Havana! A minibus will come and pick us up from the residence. This day is free to explore how you want. The Havana Team will join us and show us around should we want that. Lunch is not provided this day.

Depart back home.
