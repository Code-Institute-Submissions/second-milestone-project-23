Our Mission

We are a ministry that started with just a couple in love with travelling and serving God while out doing so. This led us to making some great contacts in several countries who kept on inviting us back. The idea sparked in us that we cando so much more if we had a team with us and started inviting people to join us in different capacities, thus making us the ministry we are today. As we are a relatively young family the trips are mainly suited for young adults or youth groups, but we do of course welcome anyone who is up for an adventure!

Matthew 28:19 says:

”Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptising them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit.”

If you want to find out more send us an email and we’ll get back to you! You can also subscribe to our newsletter if you want to keep up to date with what we’re doing.

Mission Trips

We are currently offering mission trips to 4 different countries: Cuba, Russia, Myanmar and India. The type of trip will be dependant on what country you choose as the needs vary for each of them. We believe in practicing what the Bible teaches. Mark 10:45 states that:

”For even the Son of Man did not come to be served, but to serve, and to give His life as a ransom for many.”

We have an obligation as Christians to serve God in whatever capacity we can. That’s why we want everyone who joins us to be involved in one way or another. Details on how you can be involved can be seen in each country. For youth groups we would highly recommend you join either our trip to Cuba or India. There are limited spaces on each trip so please contact us quickly to secure a space or reserve a space for the next time the trip runs!
