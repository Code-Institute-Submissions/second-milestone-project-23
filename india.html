India

10 days

India is a country known for its flavoursome food, colourful saris and chaotic traffic. Some dear friends of ours have lived and worked in Kolkata, India, for over 20 years now serving in the community where they live. They have opened up a church and offer different kinds of activities, such as guitar lessons, English lessons, pre-school etc. for families in their neighbourhood. They give their everything to serve God in any way that they can. Acts 20:35 says:

"In everything I did, I showed you that by this kind of hard work we must help the weak, remembering the words the Lord Jesus himself said: ‘It is more blessed to give than to receive.’"

And that is exactly what these missionaries do. If you want to see what it’s like to really live and serve small communities in India, I strongly recommend you to join us on this trip. Here you will get to connect with locals on a very personal level as well as serving in any way you can.
