Myanmar

8 days

Myanmar, often referred to as The Golden Land, is a beautiful country with beautiful people. The church we’re working with is a big church of over 6000 members at the moment. They run a big bible school where students from all over Myanmar come to join.

This mission is led by the Swedish missionaries who happened to start this bible school in 1996, Thord and Susanne Renkel. Here you will get to see firsthand two missionaries at work doing what God has called them to do. The bible school runs from 8:15 in the morning to 12:00. During the mornings other members of the team will be asked to share something if it’s something they are interested in.

This trip is perfect for someone who wants to get immersed into bible teaching and learn from veterans.

Arrive in Yangon and get checked in at the hotel.

The church has three different service on Sundays in three different languages. We will participate in all 3 of them.

Depart hotel at 7:45

Worship starts at 8:15. At 9 - 10 there will be a teaching session. 10:15 - 10:45 a member of the team will share something to the students. Teaching resumes at 11 and ends at 12.

Lunch with bible school staff

Afternoon is free to explore Yangon, or recover at the hotel from jetlag.

Depart hotel at 7:45

Worship starts at 8:15. At 9 - 10 there will be a teaching session. 10:15 - 10:45 a member of the team will share something to the students. Teaching resumes at 11 and ends at 12.

Lunch with bible school staff

Afternoon is free to explore Yangon, we particularly recommend Bogyoke Market. In the evening we will visit Shwe Dagon Pagoda, which is a must when visiting Myanmar.

Depart hotel at 7:45

Worship starts at 8:15. At 9 - 10 there will be a teaching session. 10:15 - 10:45 a member of the team will share something to the students. Teaching resumes at 11 and ends at 12.

Lunch with bible school staff

This afternoon we will head out to the church orphanage to see the work they are doing there as well as have dinner there with them.

Depart hotel at 7:45

Worship starts at 8:15. At 9 - 10 there will be a teaching session. 10:15 - 10:45 a member of the team will share something to the students. Teaching resumes at 11 and ends at 12.

Lunch with bible school staff

Prayer together with the church in the afternoon from 13:15 to 15:00

Depart hotel at 7:45

Worship starts at 8:15. At 9 - 10 there will be a teaching session. 10:15 - 10:45 a member of the team will share something to the students. As it is the final day we will also pray for the students.

Lunch with bible school staff

The afternoon is free to explore Yangon. In the evening we will have dinner at Karaweik Palace which is a beautiful boat on Kandawgyi lake, serving traditional Burmese food buffet as well as a traditional show.

Depart Yangon.
