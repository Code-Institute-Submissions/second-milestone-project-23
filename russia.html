Russia

6 days

This is a mini trip to St.Petersburg, Russia, where we have 5 intense days of working with the church there. The focus of this trip will be bible teaching and worship. This trip won’t be as hands on as other trips as the main teaching will come from the main team, however there will be opportunity to give testimonies and the whole team will be involved in praying for people.

This trip is for those who want to soak up some powerful bible teaching and see first hand what it means to train people. If this sounds like you, why don’t you enquire below and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible.

Arrive in St.Petersburg. Get settled in the hotel, then grab a bite to eat.

Worship workshop from 13:15 to 15:00.
